What Is A Mime ?

Mime, which was popularized around the world by The Charlie Chaplin is another act that is performed by our team. The artists also referred to as mimes paint their faces and convey a story through their actions. The performance is filled with music and energy and this energy is also reciprocated by the audience.

" Mime makes the invisible, visible and the visible, invisible."

Marcel Marceau
