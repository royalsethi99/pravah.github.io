What Is A Skit ?

This act is performed on the last day of the cultural festival and is like a Thank You to the audience. It is a comedic act which greatly revolves around the college and the college crowd. It talks about recent events and activities that take place in the college in a satirical manner which usually leaves the audience laughing off their seats.
