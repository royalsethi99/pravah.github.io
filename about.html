PRAVAH Dramatics Club NITH

PRAVAH- Dramatics Club NITH is a name which is well known for its liveliness and zest straight from the freshness of the hills. Pravah came into existence with the motto - 'Dream' 'Dare' 'Drama' and since then it has come a long way. The culture that is practiced inside the club is what gets the best out of a person and that is completely based upon mutual co-operation of the Seniors and Juniors. We work all around the year not only to act and entertain but also to raise awareness about social issues through our spellbinding performances. Be it script writing, acting, creating music or direction of the acts PRAVAH does it all. The talent and the enthusiasm that our performers contain can move you to tears or make you laugh your heart out. We welcome you to our mesmerizing world of dramatics and wish you to witness our vibrant spectacle of art.
