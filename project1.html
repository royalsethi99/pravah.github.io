What Is A Choreo ?

A brainchild of ‘Pravah’ itself, it is one of the most celebrated acts on the annual cultural festival. It is a musical act which depicts a story that is guided by music and the acting.This was initially performed in the year 2004 but has evolved a lot since then. This highlights as to how a simple story can bring out so many emotions and greatly touch the audience. Some acts that were performed in the past few years have been: ‘Dhuan’, ‘Astitva’, ‘Parvarish’, ‘Dhu’, ‘Khwaish’ and ‘Aarzoo’.
