What Is A Play ?

Theatrical Play has always been an integral part of every Drama Society across the globe and so is the case in our group. The Play is generally performed at the annual cultural festival. This act has a separate audience. It is not as fast-paced or vibrant as other acts but tries to teach the nuances of life. Pravah has been credited for some delightful plays which include: ‘Panah’, ‘Anjaam’, ‘Pyada’, ‘Ufaan’, ‘Lakeer’ and ‘Safar’. Only last year a new event called ‘Karwaan’ was organized in the college for the screening of the play ‘Safar’ in the college auditorium which was greatly appreciated by the audience.
