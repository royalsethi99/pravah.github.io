What Is A Shadow Act ?

Just as the name suggests, this act is done behind the curtain where what the audience sees are only shadows. This act is also a musical act and is a visual treat for the audience. Although, it has been performed only twice but has had characters ranging from Dragons to sharks to a whole castle. This act truly allows the performers to let the imagination run wild and this is the reason that it is well-liked by the audience.
